## Part 1: Alex Chang
### Opening · Problem · Team Intro · Handover

Good afternoon, honourable judges. It is a privilege to stand before you today to present MediSolution.

The numbers tell a stark story. As shown on this slide, public A&E waiting times for non-urgent cases regularly stretch from 4 to 8 hours. A single private GP consultation costs between $400 and $800, a full day's wages for many low-income workers. And with only 2.1 doctors per 1,000 residents, Hong Kong falls nearly 45% short of the OECD benchmark of 3.7. The result is a vicious cycle: those who need care the most are the least able to access it. They delay. They self-diagnose. They rely on unreliable information online. And in doing so, they place themselves at serious risk.

That is the problem we set out to solve. That is why we built MediSolution.

I am Alex Chang, and with me are Kathy and Alex Ho. Together, we have developed a platform with one unwavering mission: to ensure that every person in Hong Kong, regardless of their age, income, or background, has access to reliable medical guidance, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, completely free of charge.

## Part 2: Kathy
### Solution 1 + Solution 2 · Main Body · Handover

**[Solution 1: Present: What is it?]**

Thank you, Alex. The problem is clear. Now let me show you exactly how MediSolution solves it.

Our first solution is the **AI Smart Consultation**, the cornerstone of MediSolution. At its heart, it is a free, 24/7 intelligent medical chatbot that puts a reliable health companion in the pocket of every Hong Kong resident.

**[Elaborate: Explaining Concepts + How It Works]**

Here is how it works. The user opens the app and describes their symptoms in text, or uploads a photo of the affected area. Our AI then analyses the input and cross-references it against real-time data from the Hospital Authority. Within seconds, the user receives a clear risk assessment, either urgent, requiring immediate attention, or non-urgent, with a recommended course of action, alongside a preliminary health report they can bring to their doctor.

**[Elaborate: Relatable Example]**

Let me bring this to life with a real example from our pilot test. Mr Chan, a 68-year-old retired security guard living alone in Sham Shui Po, woke at 2am with chest tightness and shortness of breath. He opened MediSolution, described his symptoms, and received an immediate response: "Based on your symptoms, this may require urgent attention. Please proceed to the nearest A&E now." His daughter received an automatic alert on her phone and rushed to accompany him. He was diagnosed with a minor cardiac event, and the early response made all the difference.

**[Conclude Solution 1]**

This is not a hypothetical. Our pilot study recorded an accuracy rate of 88.6% across hundreds of medical case assessments. For the elderly and low-income families, this means trustworthy guidance at any hour, at no cost. For Hong Kong's overstretched healthcare system, it means fewer unnecessary A&E visits and more resources for those who truly need them.

---

**[Solution 2: Present: What is it?]**

Our second solution, **Smart Medical Tools**, extends this support far beyond the initial consultation. It brings together three powerful capabilities in one app.

**[Elaborate: Providing Data + Implementation Details]**

The first is Smart Map: using the phone's location, the app instantly identifies nearby hospitals and clinics, with real-time opening hours and estimated travel time. The second is Second Medical Opinion: users upload X-ray, CT, or MRI scan reports and receive a clear, plain-language explanation of the findings, cutting through the medical jargon that patients so often find overwhelming. The third is Drug Information: a simple photo of any medicine returns a full breakdown of its purpose, side effects, and critical warnings.

Every piece of information is sourced directly from the Hong Kong Department of Health and the Hospital Authority, ensuring accuracy and local relevance.

**[Elaborate: Relatable Example + Conclude Solution 2]**

For example, an elderly woman receives a CT scan report after her hospital visit. The page is filled with medical terms she cannot understand. She photographs it with MediSolution, and within moments, the app explains it to her in simple, reassuring language. She understands her condition. She knows what to do next. She is no longer afraid.

Once widely adopted, Smart Medical Tools has the potential to reach over 1.4 million elderly residents across Hong Kong, alongside countless low-income families and medical students. For those who have long felt shut out of the healthcare conversation, it gives them something invaluable: knowledge, confidence, and control.

Now, Alex Ho will address the challenges ahead — and the steps we have taken to overcome them.

## Part 3: Alex Ho
### Limitations · Future · Conclusion · Closing

**[Limitations + Follow-up Actions]**

Thank you, Kathy. Our solutions are ambitious — and we believe they are achievable. But we would be doing this panel a disservice if we did not acknowledge the challenges that lie ahead.

For the AI Smart Consultation, our greatest challenge is adoption. Some elderly users may feel intimidated by new technology, or distrust a digital tool with something as personal as their health. We have confronted this head-on. Our interface is built on a zero-learning-cost philosophy: large text, high-contrast visuals, and full voice input, so that even users with no prior experience with smartphones can navigate it confidently. We will also establish a city-wide volunteer network, partnering with community centres and elderly homes to provide hands-on setup support, ensuring that no one is left behind.

**[Privacy + Follow-up]**

Our second challenge is trust — specifically, trust around data privacy. Medical information is among the most sensitive data a person can share. MediSolution addresses this through full user control. Individuals choose where their data is stored: on their own device, or on our secure online servers. They set their own deletion schedule. We do not share their information with any third party, and we do not use it to improve our systems. Their health data belongs to them, and to them alone.

**[Future Development]**

Looking further ahead, our vision extends well beyond Hong Kong. In the near term, we aim to deepen our partnership with the Hospital Authority and the Department of Health to integrate richer, more comprehensive local health data. From there, we plan to expand into the Greater Bay Area and, ultimately, to make MediSolution available to anyone, anywhere, in any of the 11 languages our platform already supports.

**[Conclusion: 4 Elements]**

To conclude, we stand before you today not simply to present a product, but to advocate for a principle: that access to reliable healthcare guidance is not a privilege — it is a right. Our AI Smart Consultation empowers users to make informed decisions at any hour. Our Smart Medical Tools place the knowledge of a specialist in the hands of anyone with a smartphone. Together, they form a system designed to ease the burden on our hospitals, protect the financial wellbeing of our most vulnerable communities, and dismantle the wall of misinformation that stands between people and their health. MediSolution is our commitment to a Hong Kong where no one faces a medical crisis alone.

**[Closing]**

Thank you sincerely for your time and attention today. Your expertise and insights would be enormously valuable to us as we move forward. We look forward to your questions and feedback. Thank you very much.

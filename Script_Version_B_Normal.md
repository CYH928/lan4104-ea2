## Part 1: Alex Chang
### Opening · Problem · Team Intro · Handover

**[Greeting]**

Good afternoon, honourable judges. It is our pleasure to present MediSolution today, and we greatly appreciate your time and attention.

**[Pain Point 1 – Emotional Appeal]**

Picture a typical night in Hong Kong. An elderly man sits alone in a small flat at 3 in the morning, gripped by a sudden, sharp pain in his chest. Fear sets in. Should he call an ambulance? Should he endure the pain until dawn? With no one to guide him and no trustworthy resource at hand, he turns to an online search, only to be met with conflicting, unreliable information. In moments like these, many elderly residents and low-income families are left without access to timely medical guidance, unable to distinguish what is truly urgent from what can wait. The result is not just physical suffering, but deep anxiety and a profound sense of helplessness.

**[Pain Point 2 – Logical Appeal]**

This challenge is compounded by alarming structural gaps in our healthcare system. As shown on this slide, the waiting time at Hong Kong's public A&E departments can stretch from 4 to 8 hours for non-urgent cases. A single private GP visit costs anywhere from $400 to over $800. Furthermore, Hong Kong has only 2.1 doctors per 1,000 residents, significantly below the OECD benchmark of 3.7. As a result, many residents delay seeking care, rely on misinformation, or forgo treatment altogether. It is therefore deeply concerning that this healthcare gap disproportionately impacts our most vulnerable populations.

That is why we are here today: to present MediSolution, a platform designed to bridge this critical gap.

As a team of Higher Diploma students in Cloud and Data Centre Administration, our passion lies in applying AI and cloud technology to solve real-world problems. We are the MediSolution team. I am Alex Chang, and with me are Kathy and Alex Ho. Our mission is to provide every person in Hong Kong with free, intelligent medical guidance, available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Today, we will first introduce our AI Smart Consultation, then our Smart Medical Tools, before addressing our challenges and conclusion. Now, my teammate Kathy will explain how our solutions bring this mission to life.

## Part 2: Kathy
### Solution 1 + Solution 2 · Main Body · Handover

**[Solution 1: Present: What is it?]**

Thank you for the introduction, Alex. Let me now walk you through our two proposed solutions.

Our first solution is the **AI Smart Consultation**, a free, round-the-clock AI-powered medical chatbot, accessible through MediSolution on any device, anytime, anywhere.

**[Elaborate: Explaining Concepts + How It Works]**

Allow me to explain how it works. First, the user opens the app and enters their symptoms in text, or uploads a photo of the affected area. Then, our AI analyses the input and cross-references it with real-time data from the Hospital Authority to assess the medical situation. Finally, the app generates a clear risk assessment, either urgent or non-urgent, along with a concise preliminary health report. This entire process requires no prior technical knowledge, and no additional effort from the user.

**[Elaborate: Relatable Example]**

Here is a real example from our pilot test. An elderly gentleman experienced chest discomfort late at night. He opened MediSolution, described his symptoms, and within seconds received the assessment: "This is likely non-urgent. We recommend consulting a GP tomorrow morning." Rather than enduring a night of fear or spending 8 hours at A&E, he was able to rest with confidence. His daughter, who lives across town, received an automatic notification and called him right away.

**[Conclude Solution 1]**

As evidenced by our pilot study, our AI achieved an accuracy rate of 88.6% in medical case assessments. For elderly users and low-income families, this means round-the-clock access to trustworthy guidance at no cost. For Hong Kong's healthcare system, it represents a meaningful reduction in non-urgent A&E visits.

---

**[Solution 2: Present: What is it?]**

Moving on to our second solution, **Smart Medical Tools**, which integrates three powerful features into a single, user-friendly app.

**[Elaborate: Providing Data + Implementation Details]**

The first feature is our Smart Map, which uses GPS to locate the nearest hospitals and clinics, displaying real-time opening hours and estimated travel time. The second is Second Medical Opinion, allowing users to upload diagnostic scans, including X-ray, CT, and MRI reports, and receive a plain-language explanation of complex medical terminology. The third is Drug Information, where users photograph any medication to instantly retrieve its functions, side effects, and usage warnings.

To ensure accuracy, we have integrated authoritative data from the Hong Kong Department of Health and the Hospital Authority. With our working prototype already operational, rollout would require minimal adjustments from partner institutions.

**[Elaborate: Relatable Example + Conclude Solution 2]**

For example, a patient receives a CT scan report filled with complex medical terminology. She uploads it to MediSolution, and within moments receives a clear, plain-language explanation, which reduces her anxiety and empowers her to make informed decisions. Once fully implemented city-wide, this solution has the potential to serve over 1.4 million elderly residents in Hong Kong, as well as countless low-income families and medical students.

## Part 3: Alex Ho
### Limitations · Future · Conclusion · Closing

**[Limitations + Follow-up Actions]**

Thank you, Kathy. While our two solutions are innovative and highly promising, we recognise that they may face certain challenges.

For the AI Smart Consultation, a primary concern is that some elderly users may initially feel overwhelmed by unfamiliar technology. To address this, we have designed our interface with a zero-learning-cost approach, featuring large text, high-contrast display, and voice input to minimise barriers. In addition, we will partner with community centres and elderly homes to conduct hands-on setup sessions, supported by trained volunteers who will guide users through the experience.

**[Privacy + Follow-up]**

Furthermore, we understand that some users may have concerns about the privacy and security of their medical data. To overcome this, MediSolution puts users in full control of their own data. They may choose to keep all information stored only on their own device, or use our secure online storage option. Users set their own data deletion schedule, and we guarantee that their information is never shared with any third party, and never used to train our AI models.

**[Future Development]**

Looking ahead, we plan to deepen our collaboration with the Hospital Authority and the Department of Health to further enrich our local medical dataset. Our roadmap also includes expanding into the Greater Bay Area, followed by a global rollout, a vision that our existing 11-language app is well-positioned to support.

**[Conclusion: 4 Elements]**

To conclude, we envision a future where every Hong Kong resident has access to reliable, affordable medical guidance, regardless of the time, their income, or their background. Our AI Smart Consultation and Smart Medical Tools work together to make this vision a reality. Together, they are designed to ease the burden on our public healthcare system, significantly reduce out-of-pocket medical costs for vulnerable communities, and effectively combat the spread of dangerous medical misinformation. Through collaboration with the Hospital Authority, government agencies, and community organisations, MediSolution is committed to making quality healthcare guidance a right, not a privilege, for every person in Hong Kong.

**[Closing]**

Thank you very much for the opportunity to present our work. We greatly appreciate your time and attention, and your insights will be invaluable in strengthening our strategies. We warmly welcome your questions and feedback. Thank you very much.

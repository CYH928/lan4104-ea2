## Part 1: Alex Chang
### Opening · Problem · Team Intro · Handover

**[Greeting]**

Good afternoon, honourable judges. Thank you for taking the time to listen to our presentation today.

**[Pain Point 1 – Emotional Appeal]**

Picture a typical night in Hong Kong. An elderly man lives alone in a small flat. It is 3 in the morning. He suddenly feels pain in his chest. He is scared and confused. Should he call an ambulance? Should he wait until morning? There is no doctor to call at this hour. He tries to search online, but the information is confusing and hard to trust. In the end, he sits alone in the dark, waiting, and hoping for the best. Many elderly people and low-income families in Hong Kong face this kind of situation every day. They feel worried, helpless, and they do not know where to turn for help.

**[Pain Point 2 – Logical Appeal]**

This is a serious problem that affects many people in our city. As you can see on this slide, the waiting time at public A&E in Hong Kong can be 4 to 8 hours for non-urgent cases. A single visit to a private doctor costs $400 to $800 or more. And Hong Kong only has 2.1 doctors for every 1,000 people, which is much less than the OECD world standard of 3.7. Because of these problems, many people cannot get help when they need it most. They wait too long, spend money they cannot afford, or find no trustworthy information online. This means the healthcare gap is real, and it is growing.

That is why we want to find a better way. That is why we created MediSolution.

As a group of Higher Diploma students in Cloud and Data Centre Administration, our team wants to use our knowledge in AI and cloud technology to help people. We are the MediSolution team. I am Alex Chang. With me today are Kathy and Alex Ho. Our mission is simple: to give every person in Hong Kong access to free, reliable medical guidance, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

In this presentation, we will first explain our AI Smart Consultation, then introduce our Smart Medical Tools, and finally discuss our challenges and conclusion. Now, my teammate Kathy will explain how our solutions work.

## Part 2: Kathy
### Solution 1 + Solution 2 · Main Body · Handover

**[Solution 1: Present: What is it?]**

Thank you, Alex. I will start by talking about our first solution.

Our first solution is called **AI Smart Consultation**. It is a free, 24/7 AI chatbot that anyone can use on their phone at any time of the day.

**[Elaborate: Explaining How It Works]**

Let me explain how it works. First, the user opens the MediSolution app and types their symptoms, or takes a photo of the affected area. Then, our AI checks the information and looks up real-time hospital data from the Hospital Authority. Finally, the app gives a risk level, either urgent or non-urgent, and creates a simple preliminary health report for the user. Everything happens automatically, with no special knowledge needed.

**[Elaborate: Relatable Example]**

For example, imagine our elderly man from earlier. He feels chest pain at 3 in the morning. He opens MediSolution, types his symptoms, and within seconds the app tells him: "This is likely non-urgent. We suggest you visit a GP in the morning." He feels calm and safe. He does not need to rush to the A&E or wait 8 hours in a hospital.

**[Conclude Solution 1]**

In our test, the AI correctly assessed 88.6% of cases. For elderly users, this means they can get help at any time, for free. For our healthcare system, this means fewer people going to A&E for non-urgent problems.

---

**[Solution 2: Present: What is it?]**

Next, I will introduce our second solution: **Smart Medical Tools**. This solution has three parts in one app.

**[Elaborate: Providing Data + Implementation Details]**

The first part is our Smart Map. The app uses GPS to find the nearest hospital or clinic, with opening hours and travel time shown clearly. The second part is Second Medical Opinion. Users can upload their X-ray, CT scan, or MRI report, and the AI explains the medical terms in simple language. The third part is Drug Information. Users take a photo of any medicine, and the app tells them what it does, the side effects, and any important warnings.

To make sure the information is correct, we use trusted data from the Hong Kong Department of Health and the Hospital Authority.

**[Elaborate: Relatable Example + Conclude Solution 2]**

For example, a patient receives a CT scan report full of difficult medical words. She uploads it to MediSolution, and within seconds the app explains it in plain language. She feels less worried and knows what to do next. If we apply this solution city-wide, it could help over 1.4 million elderly residents in Hong Kong, and many more low-income families and medical students.

Now, Alex Ho will talk about our challenges, future plans, and conclusion.

## Part 3: Alex Ho
### Limitations · Future · Conclusion · Closing

**[Limitations + Follow-up Actions]**

Thank you, Kathy. Even though our two solutions are useful, they also have some challenges.

For our AI Smart Consultation, some elderly users may feel scared or confused by new technology. To deal with this, we have designed our app with a zero-learning-cost interface, with large text, high contrast display, and voice input. Users just speak into the phone and the app understands them. We will also send volunteers to community centres and elderly homes to help people set up the app and feel confident using it.

**[Privacy + Follow-up]**

For our Smart Medical Tools, some users may worry about their privacy. Medical information is very personal. To protect our users, we give them full control over their data. They can choose to save their information only on their own phone, or use our secure online storage. Users can also set a timer to delete their data after a few days. We never share any user data with other companies, and we never use it to train our AI.

**[Future Development]**

In the future, we hope to work more closely with the Hong Kong government and the Hospital Authority to add more local healthcare data. We also plan to expand to the Greater Bay Area and, one day, to the whole world. Our app already supports 11 languages, so we are ready for this next step.

**[Conclusion: 4 Elements]**

To sum up, our AI Smart Consultation helps users get free, reliable medical advice anytime, and our Smart Medical Tools help them find clinics, understand medical reports, and learn about their medicines. Together, these two solutions can reduce pressure on public hospitals, lower medical costs for low-income families, and fight false medical information online. We believe that with MediSolution, every person in Hong Kong, no matter their age, income, or background, can get the healthcare guidance they deserve.

**[Closing]**

Thank you very much for your attention and for this opportunity. Your insights and feedback will be invaluable to us. We are very happy to answer any questions you may have. Thank you.
